About Me

My name is Alicia M. Begay, I was born in Gallup, New Mexico raised on the Navajo reservation know as Twin Buttes, located southwest of Gallup. As a member of the Navajo Nation my introduction includes my 4 clans (maternal and paternal). so here it goes. Ya'ta'hey, Haltsooi Dine' (Meadow People) nishli, Kinyaa'aanii bashishchiin (Towering HouseP People), Tl'aashchi'i (Red Cheek People) dashicheii, Tse'nahabilnii' (Sleep-Rock People)dashinali'.

I am a mother, daugther, sister, aunt, and grandmother. My daughter is almost 13 yrs old, and is starting to put a new face on since she is now in middle school. I live near my mother and sister. My mother is the most amazing loving, supporting person I will ever know, she is my rock. Without her I would not be the person I am today! My sister I love dearly since she is my one and only. Thankfully she has 5 children that I adore just as much as I do my own. They all keep me on my toes especially my grandgirls whom are still babies, they keep me an awe for how they have changed our small family with growth and
